Plot

Hello, Love, Goodbye follows Joy, a domestic helper in Hong Kong, and Ethan, a bartender. As they navigate their struggles and aspirations, they develop an emotional connection that challenges their dreams and personal choices.

Key Information

  • Genre: Romance, Drama
  • Director: Cathy Garcia-Molina
  • Release Year: 2019
  • Language: Filipino, English
  • Box Office: Over $17 million

Main Cast

  • Kathryn Bernardo as Joy
  • Alden Richards as Ethan
  • Maymay Entrata as Mary Dale
  • Joross Gamboa as Jhim
  • Jameson Blake as Marcus

Characters

Joy is a hardworking domestic helper striving for a better future, while Ethan is a carefree bartender learning about commitment and love.

Reviews

The film received critical acclaim for its heartfelt performances and realistic portrayal of overseas Filipino workers. It became the highest-grossing Filipino film of its time.
